The **Semantics** ( _process_ ) section focuses on the meaning and comprehension of programming constructs. Building on the understanding of syntax, this section delves deeper into **program literacy**—interpreting what programs mean and how they operate. It transitions learners into the **technique stage**, where the emphasis is on reading, understanding, and analyzing code as a process of computation.
